Alizée Bocian

FullStack Developer Trainee

At the begining

Formerly a school teacher, I joined a web development bootcamp @BeCode. I encountered barriers in my practice and wanted to develop an app to optimize my work. It was while discussing the feasibility of the project with a developer that... "oh! God! I will never look back! I was definitively hooked, made for this... and now determined to dedicate the rest of my career to it!

First steps

During my training, I quickly became passionate about backend development. I only had the opportunity to create one project in Python during which I became familiar with OOP. Second crush. Code that is more solid yet modifiable, better structured, more efficient, and faster to debug. The desire to delve into this kind of language has now become a certainty.

And tomorrow?

Continue learning! That's the goal I set for myself from my first day at BeCode: to find an employer who, despite my technical level, will have confidence in my learning abilities, ready to entrust me with responsibilities to see me progress rapidly within a dynamic and highly skilled team. I am motivated and resourceful. It's all a matter of opportunities!

What about Software Development?

Software development is at the core of what drives and stimulates me. It allows me, unlike web development, to invest my skills in durable, inherently useful products. It allows me to support projects with major societal impact by improving or facilitating the daily lives of both businesses and citizens. It fulfills me intellectually due to the necessity to analyze and solve real-world problems in an environment that is constantly changing.
